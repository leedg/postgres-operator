# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We pledge to provide a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless
of gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, disability,
personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or
nationality.

## Standards

Behavior that creates a positive environment:

- Welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect for differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Graceful acceptance of constructive criticism.
- A focus on what is best for the community as a whole.
- Empathy toward other community members.

Behavior that is not tolerated:

- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political
  attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information (address, email, etc.) without
  explicit permission.
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to `coc@keiailab.io`. All reports will be
reviewed and acted on as appropriate; the identity of the reporter is kept
confidential.

[Contributor Covenant homepage](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
